In "A Word Game" you are presented with a psuedo-randomly generated string of characters
that you are expected to make words out of.
You enter your word in the text box at the top, and hit enter to submit.
If you get stuck, you can always press the (newly revamped) redraw button to get
a different string of characters. Your goal is to have more words than redraws.

It's written in Java 8, so you'll need it
in order to play.
